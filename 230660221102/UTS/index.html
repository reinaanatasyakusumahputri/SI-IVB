About Me

Hello! I am Jajang Komara, an Information Systems student at Sebelas April University (UNSAP) with a strong passion for graphic design. I believe that a good system should not only function optimally, but also be pleasing to the eye and easy to use.

Outside of class, I am active as a member of UKM UNSAP Radio. This experience not only honed my communication and teamwork skills, but also gave me the opportunity to apply and develop my graphic design skills through creating visual content for both digital and offline platforms. I am always enthusiastic about learning new things and looking for opportunities where I can combine my interests in technology and design. Let's connect and explore my projects!
